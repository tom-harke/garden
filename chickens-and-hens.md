# Chickens and Hens - *Sempervivum*

Succulent perennials that form mats of rosettes. Extremely drought-tolerant and perfect for the "Brick Wall" zone.

## Characteristics
*   **Growth Habit:** Clumping rosettes that produce "chicks" on runners.
*   **Sun:** Full sun.
*   **Water:** Very low once established.
*   **Soil:** Requires excellent drainage.
