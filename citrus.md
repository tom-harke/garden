= Citrus
== Zone 8b/9a

Can survive out doors
 - yuzu
 - some satsumas
 - some blood oranges

Cannot:
 - lime
 - lemon

== The Plan
Constraints
 - wife likes the privacy of existing arbor vitae on property line

Actions
 - on existing arbor vitae trim branches where new tree is to go, on south side
    - as citrus tree grows, gradually trim more
    - trim all branches
    - leave trunks for a few years, to support tarp in case of winter cold snap
 - even later
    - cut off trunks at base
    - don't dig up -- just leave to decay in place
