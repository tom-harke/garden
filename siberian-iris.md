# Siberian Iris - *Iris sibirica*

Graceful, moisture-loving irises with elegant blooms and grass-like foliage.

## Care in Zone 8b/9a
*   **Light:** Morning sun and filtered afternoon shade.
*   **Soil:** Rich, fertile, and slightly acidic (pH 5.5–6.5).
*   **Water:** Unlike bearded irises, these **love moisture**. Keep soil consistently moist throughout the growing season.
*   **Chill Factor:** Needs a period of winter cold to trigger heavy blooming.

## Propagation
*   **Division:** The easiest and most common method; divide the root clumps in early spring as new growth appears or in late summer after flowering.
*   **Rhizomes:** Ensure each division has at least one healthy fan of leaves and a strong section of rhizome with roots.

## Garden Uses
*   Excellent for rain gardens or pond margins.
*   Upright foliage provides vertical interest all season.
*   Pairs well with ferns, hostas, and azaleas.
