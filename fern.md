# Ferns

A collection of ferns for the forest zone, providing texture and year-round green in deep shade.

## Varieties in the Garden
*   **Christmas Fern (*Polystichum acrostichoides*):** A clump-forming evergreen fern native to eastern North America.
*   **Maidenhair Fern (*Adiantum*):** Delicate, fan-shaped fronds on thin black stems.
