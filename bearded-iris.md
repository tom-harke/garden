# Bearded Iris - *Iris germanica*

Rugged perennials known for their dramatic blooms and "beards."

## Care in Zone 8b/9a
The primary challenge in this zone is intense summer heat and humidity.

*   **Sunlight:** Prefers **morning sun and afternoon shade**. 6 hours of direct morning sun is ideal to prevent rhizomes from scorching.
*   **Planting Depth:** Cover the rhizome with **1 inch of soil** to protect it from the sun (unlike cooler zones where they are left exposed).
*   **Soil:** Requires exceptional drainage. Prone to rhizome rot in wet or heavy soil. Raised beds are highly recommended.
*   **Maintenance:** Divide every 3-4 years in late September or October.

## Propagation
* **Division:** Primary method; divide rhizomes every 3-4 years in late summer/autumn.
* **Seeds:** Possible but slow; requires cold stratification and may not be true to parent.
* **Cuttings:** Not possible.

## Benefits
*   Classic ornamental beauty.
*   Drought tolerant once established.
