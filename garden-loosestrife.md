# Garden Loosestrife - *Lysimachia punctata*

A hardy, upright perennial (2-3 ft) that thrives in moist, sunny to partially shaded garden beds.

## Characteristics
*   **Flowers:** Whorls of bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer.
*   **Garden Use:** Excellent for the back of borders, cottage gardens, and boggy areas near water features.
*   **Management:** Highly vigorous spreader via underground rhizomes. Plant in containers or use barriers to control growth.

## Care (Zone 8b/9a)
*   **Light:** Full sun to partial shade.
*   **Soil:** Prefers consistently moist, humus-rich soil.
*   **Pollinators:** Provides a nectar source during the mid-summer bloom gap.

## Propagation
* **Division:** Easiest method; divide rhizomatous clumps in spring or autumn.
* **Cuttings:** Highly successful; 4-6" stem cuttings or basal/root cuttings.
* **Seeds:** Sow in autumn or spring in moist soil.
